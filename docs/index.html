About this app

SmartPark app is the official smart parking assistant designed for Universitas Internasional Batam. Streamline your campus parking experience with live real-time spot vacancy mapping, fast ticketed entry QR gates, automatic parking session expiry reminder alerts (15 minutes warning), and a dynamic occupancy flow simulator.

No more driving in circles searching for spots. SmartPark keeps you informed, on time, and parked safely.

Data safety

Safety starts with understanding how developers collect and share your data.
No data shared with third parties The developer declares that this app does not share user data with other companies or organizations.
Data is encrypted in transit Your credentials and parking schedules are sent over a secure connection to Google Firebase.
